Shoot one light ray through image pixel (col, row)

The parameters (col, row) are measured in the same way as they are in :class:.HdrImage: the bottom left corner is placed at (0, 0). The values of u_pixel and v_pixel are floating-point numbers in the range [0, 1]. They specify where the ray should cross the pixel; passing 0.5 to both means that the ray will pass through the pixel's center.
