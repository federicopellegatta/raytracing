Detailed Description

A class representing an intersection between a ray and a shape.

Parameters
world_pointa :class:.Point object holding the world coordinates of the hit point
normala :class:.Normal object holding the orientation of the normal to the surface where the hit happened
surface_pointa :class:.Vec2d object holding the position of the hit point on the surface of the object
ta floating-point value specifying the distance from the origin of the ray where the hit happened
raythe ray that hit the surface
hita bool which says whether the ray hit the surface
shapepointer to the shape hit by the ray
